Types of Fraud We Detect

FraudShield identifies and categorizes these common digital fraud patterns.

🎣

Phishing Attacks

Fraudulent emails, SMS, or calls impersonating banks, government agencies, or trusted services to steal credentials.

👤

Identity Theft

Unauthorized use of personal documents like Aadhaar or PAN to open accounts, take loans, or commit crimes.

💰

Financial Scams

Lottery wins, prize money, or investment schemes requiring upfront payments or bank details.

🧠

Social Engineering

Psychological manipulation tactics to influence victims into revealing sensitive information or taking harmful actions.

📈

Investment Fraud

Fake high-return investment schemes, Ponzi structures, or unregulated crypto platforms promising guaranteed returns.

💳

UPI & Payment Fraud

Fake payment requests, QR code scams, and UPI fraud tactics targeting digital payment users.
