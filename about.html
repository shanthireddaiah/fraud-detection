About FraudShield

We believe every citizen deserves access to tools that help them identify and report digital fraud — without barriers, without complexity, and without cost.

🎯

Our Mission

To democratize fraud detection by delivering a frictionless, accessible platform that empowers individuals to recognize, report, and respond to digital fraud regardless of their technical background.

👁

Our Vision

A digital ecosystem where fraud awareness is universal, reporting is effortless, and communities are equipped with the knowledge and tools to protect themselves and others.

🔑

Guest-First Philosophy

We believe in earning trust before requesting data. Every visitor can access our detection tools and educational content freely — authentication is only required when submitting formal reports.

📊

Data Transparency

This is a frontend MVP demonstration. All data is stored locally in your browser via localStorage. No personal information is transmitted to any server. This platform is for demonstration and awareness purposes only.
